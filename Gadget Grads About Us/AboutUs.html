Gadget Grads is an online retail company set up by students who believe other students should have access to affordable technology, assisting in their studies. We offer a range of products suited to studies along with other technological products at a discounted price. We work closely with our retailers to ensure our products come from brands who want to support our customers. We purchase these items in bulk from our retailers and return them to you at a discounted price, 25% of our earnings go towards charity. We selected the name "Gadget Grads" as we want our customers to graduate with better tech. Our logo (twin mountain peaks) is symbolic of climbing the academic ladder, graduating to higher peaks and moving forward in life is what we stand for. Gadget Grads, graduate with better tech!
